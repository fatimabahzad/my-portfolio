2D Space Cowgirl Game

A pixel-art 2D game built in 12 hours at a hackathon with the theme "Cowboys & Space." You play as a space cowgirl trying to catch teleporting aliens using a lasso before they shoot you down with fireballs. Built entirely with Python and Pygame.

Python Pygame Pixel Art Game Design

Gameplay Video

Features

  • Lasso mechanic — click to throw toward aliens
  • Aliens teleport around the screen to dodge you
  • Aliens shoot fireballs at the cowgirl
  • 3 lives with heart display
  • Score tracker and game over screen
  • Background music and sound effects
